Why choose The Surfing Project ?

Unexpensive

We put an entire surf lesson into print that we’ll give you for free. It covers everything from beach conditions to paddling, to getting to your feet and surfing as well as safety tips and helpful information on surfboards, wetsuits, rip currents and more.

Relaxation

Flexibility, body control, and conscious breathing are essential for progressive surfing. According to the ancient yogic text, the Yogasutra of Patanjali, Yoga means "The waves of thoughts come to rest in the spirit." The same will happen to you while you are in the waves of the ocean.

Community

Surf culture includes the people, language, fashion, and lifestyle surrounding the sport of surfing. It has affected music, fashion, literature, film, art, and youth jargon in popular culture. The number of surfers throughout the world continues to increase as the culture spreads.
